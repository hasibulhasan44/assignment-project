Our Donate Plan

We work with some motive. We not only fundraise but also work to shelter people after disasters.
Besides we have to pay attention to diseases and medicines.

Food for People

The most challenging work during a disaster is to ensure food for everyone. Despite having difficulties we try to reach as many as people we can and feed them.

Medicine

In that hazy situation, many fell ill. Especially when there is a flood, diarrhoea spreads faster and terribly. To face this type of situation, we need to keep as much medicine as we can in our collection.

Rehabilitation

After a horrible rampage of disaster, everyone needs rehabilitation. It's very important. Man, domesticated animals, every affected creature needs to go back to their own address.
